How to Play

Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors by clicking on one of the images below. The computer will also make a choice, and the winner will be determined by the following rules:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock

The first player to reach 5 points wins the game. Good luck!
